Chinese Egg Cake


Image Source


The Chinese egg cake is a traditional dessert that is made to celebrate many Chinese holidays. The fluffy texture of the cake is different from a regular sponge cake you get at your local bakery. It has a strong yellow color from the eggs with a strong egg flavor. I got this recipe from my mom, who makes a lot of traditional chinese desserts for holidays and other occasions. This recipe only calls for 6 ingredients! It is simple yet delicious! You can probably make this recipe without even going to the grocery store. It calls for very simple ingredients that you may already have. With a little time and effort, you'll have a fresh plate of delicious egg cake in no time!




Total Time: 45 min

Servings: ~10

Ingredients:




Ingredient: Amount:
Eggs 6
Glutinous Rice Flour 2 Cups
All Purpose Flour 1/2 Cup
Baking Powder 2 Tablespoons
Sugar 1 Cup
Oil/Butter 2 Tablespoons

Directions:



  1. Grease a cake pan with oil or butter. Place a rack into a wok or pan that can accomodate the cake pan you are using. Fill the pot/wok with enough water till it reaches 2 inches below the rack. Boil the water on medium heat.

  2. Sift the glutinous rice flour, all purpose flour, and baking powder into a bowl. Mix the dry ingredients together.

  3. Crack the eggs into a separate bowl and whisk them together while adding sugar into the mixture. Combine the ingredients until everything is incorporated and a smooth mixture forms.

  4. Pour the mixture into the cake pan. To get rid of any air bubbles, tap the pan on the table. Set it ontop of the rack.

  5. Set a timer for approximately 30 minutes. Once the time is up, use a toothpick to check if it is cooked. If residue sticks onto the toothpick, leave it on medium heat for another 10 minutes. Lastly, serve and enjoy!
