Soft Swerve

Located in the heart of Chinatown in Manhattan, Soft Swerve is one of the most popular ice cream shops in the area. Everyday, the small store is packed with costumers waiting to try their signature flavor: Ube! Ube is a purple yam that is oftened used in Filipino desserts. Soft Swerve offers a variety of different ice cream flavors: Ube, Matcha, Black Seaseme, Coconut and other seasonal flavors.
