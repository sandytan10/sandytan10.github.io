Flourless Banana Pancakes


Image Source


These flourless banana pancakes are delicious and healthy! My sisters are really big on eating healthy and they came up with this recipe after many Sunday mornings trying out different recipes. Although it may not sound delicious, I can assure you that they are ten times better than the usual iHop pancakes without the guilt of eating so much sugar early in the morning. Not only is it healthy and delicious, but it's so simple that even I can't go wrong with making it.




Total Time: 30 min

Yields: 10 Pancakes

Ingredients:




Ingredient: Amount:
Dried Rolled Oats 1 Cup
Ripe Bananas 2
Eggs 2
Salt A pinch
Optional: Fruits, Chocolate Chips, Syrup As much as your heart desires

Directions:



  1. Place the cup of rolled oats into a blender and blend it until it becomes a fine flour texture. It doesn't need to be too grounded up.

  2. Add in 2 ripe bananas, 2 eggs, and a pinch of salt into the blender. Blend the mixture until it is smooth. It should be similar a thick smoothie.

  3. Pour the mixture into a bowl and fold in any additional fruits or ingredients that you like. You can go wild with this step!

  4. Melt some butter in a shallow pan on low heat. Pour 2 tablespoons of the pancake mixture onto the pan carefully.

  5. Once bubbles start to arise, flip the pancake to the other side and repeat. Serve with your favorite toppings and enjoy!
