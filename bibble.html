Bibble and Sip




Image Source



Bibble and Sip is a dessert shop dedicated to all types of cute pastries. They make tarts, macaroons, cream puffs, and a lot of other pastries. The amount of detail and effort put into each dessert really shines through in the presentation of their products. They're located in Midtown, Manhattan, and recently opened a shop in Hester Street, lower Manhattan. Their cream puffs are by far the best I've ever had in NYC and it never fails to dissapoint. Although they only have 2 flavors, thier Earl Grey and Matcha flavored cream puffs are bursting with flavor.



Image by Bibble and Sip via Facebook



The desserts at Bibble and Sip are on the more expensive side, but for the quality and effort that it takes to create their desserts, it is worth the hefty price. Their macaroons are beyond adorable and thier large variety garuntees that there's something for everyone. If I were you, I would definitely stop by one of their locations today and get my hands on some of their mouth watering pastries.



Location: 253 W 51st St, New York, NY 10019
