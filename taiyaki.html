Taiyaki




Image by tanitotoro via Yelp



Taiyaki is one of the most popular dessert shops in Manhattan. They have become Instagram famous for their aesthetic taiyaki cones with fillings and generous swirl of ice cream. Opened in 2016, this store never fails to amaze their customers with thier eye-opening creations. Taiyaki is a fish shaped cone that originates from Japan. It's origins stretch as far back as the 1600s in relation to a king fish called Tai. Taiyaki has done extremely well in the past 3 years and have expanded to locations to Toronto, Boston, and Miami.



Image by Taiyaki NYC via Thrillist



Recently, Taiyaki has joined the craze for jiggly souffle Japanese pancakes and have already built a long following of fans. The internet has blown up with videos and articles about insanely fluffy Japanese souffle pancakes and they have finally made their introduction in the US. Taiyaki took up this challenge and now every weekend, you can get your fill of souffle pancakes at their Chinatown location in Manhattan. If you're in Chinatown, Manhattan, definitely pop into Taiyaki and try their infamous fish shaped cones and get your fill of jiggly pancakes!



Location: 119 Baxter St, New York, NY 10013
